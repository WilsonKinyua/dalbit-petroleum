Who We Are

Dalbit Petroleum Limited (Dalbit) is a member of the Janus Continental Group (JCG). Established in 2002 in Kenya, we supply and distribute petroleum products and services across East, Central and Southern Africa.

We never back down from a challenge. Our robust logistics capability enables us to provide services to some of the most challenging and remote locations in the African continent. Our local expertise is what has established us as a trusted and reliable player among global petroleum giants in the region.
