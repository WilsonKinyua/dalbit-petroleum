Congo Petrol

Congo Petrol SARL was incorporated in 1997 and has been a Dalbit Petroleum licensee from 2009, overseeing the operations in the Democratic Republic of Congo.

Congo Petrol has a well-organized supply chain sourcing products through the ports of Dar es Salaam in Tanzania and Beira in Mozambique. Strategically located at the Lubumbashi International Airport and near Aéroport International de Goma, the company reliably offers aviation services round the clock. Our high-capacity storage facilities and solid supply - logistics network across the country enable us to efficiently support the local minining industry and other sectors.

DILSS Petroleum

DILSS Petroleum (Previously Dalbit International Limited, South Sudan), is a privately owned company incorporated in Juba South Sudan in 2003. DILSS was started as a joint venture between the Dalbit Petroleum Limited (Kenya) and Civicon Limited. The company sources a wide range of petroleum products which includes; Jet A1, Diesel, Petrol and Lubricants.

DILSS is involved in the procurement of petroleum products from the major international oil markets, import and transit of the products to its customers in the Southern Sudan region through the port of Mombasa. DILSS has a wide distribution network covering a large part of the Southern Sudan including Central, East and Western Equatoria states, Lakes State, Warrap, Western and Northern Bahr El Ghazal States. The products that are imported either from the South or the North can be stored in any of the three company’s Storage and distribution depots at Rumbek, Wau or Juba.

The company serves a diverse customer base which includes various NGO agencies, resellers and other industrial & commercial sector consumers in Aviation and road transport sectors.
